🧭 Career Guidance
Your Roadmap
After B.Sc.

Clear, honest guidance on the best paths available to life sciences students in India today.

01
IIT JAM Biotechnology
Get into IIT/IISc for M.Sc. — the most respected academic route for life sciences students.
Start preparation in 2nd semester
Focus on Molecular Bio + Chemistry
Solve 10 years of past papers
Apply in final year of B.Sc.
02
CSIR-NET Life Sciences
Qualify for JRF fellowship and lectureship. Opens doors to research labs and teaching careers.
Eligible after B.Sc. + M.Sc.
JRF gives ₹31,000/month stipend
Focus on cell biology and genetics
Exam held twice a year
03
Biotech Industry Jobs
Join pharma, diagnostics, or biotech startups directly. Growing sector with strong demand for freshers.
Do internships in 2nd/3rd year
Learn bioinformatics tools
Build LinkedIn profile early
Target Sun Pharma, Biocon, Dr. Reddy's
04
MBA in Healthcare / AI
Combine biology with business. Growing demand for people who understand both science and management.
Prepare for CAT/GMAT after B.Sc.
Target XLRI, TISS, IIHMR
Healthcare management is booming
AI + Bio = rare combo employers want
