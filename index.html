🗺 Career Guidance
10 Career Paths
After B.Sc. Biology

Clear roadmaps with key exams, skills, and action steps — tailored for Indian biology students from B.Sc. to their dream career.

01
Research Scientist (Academia)
Pursue a PhD at IITs/IISc and contribute to cutting-edge biological research. The classic scientist path.
Clear CSIR-NET or GATE (Life Sciences)
Apply to IIT/IISc/IISER PhD programmes
Publish research papers, build lab skills
Postdoc → Faculty / Senior Scientist
02
Bioinformatics Specialist
Merge biology with computing. Analyse genomes, design pipelines, and shape the future of personalised medicine.
Learn Python, R, Linux basics
Clear GATE (BT) or IIT JAM Biotech
Work with BLAST, Galaxy, AlphaFold
M.Sc. Bioinformatics → Industry / Research
03
IIT JAM → M.Sc. Path
Gateway to India's best science institutions. Opens doors to PhD, research roles and top jobs.
Prepare Biological Sciences / Biotech paper
Master Cell Biology, Genetics, Biochemistry
Mock tests + PYQ practice
IIT/NIT M.Sc. → PhD or Industry
04
Pharmaceutical / Biotech Industry
Join India's ₹4 lakh crore pharma industry as a QC analyst, formulation scientist or regulatory expert.
M.Sc. Biochemistry / Microbiology / Biotech
Learn GMP, GLP, regulatory affairs
Intern at Biocon, Cipla, Sun Pharma
QC/QA Analyst → Senior Scientist
05
CSIR-NET → JRF / Lectureship
Qualify India's most prestigious science exam. Get funded PhD research or college teaching positions.
Target December / June CSIR-NET exam
Study Life Sciences systematically
JRF: ₹37k/month fellowship for PhD
LS: Assistant Professor in colleges
06
Clinical Research Associate
Manage clinical trials and drug development pipelines. A rapidly growing career in India's CRO sector.
M.Sc. or PG Diploma in Clinical Research
Learn ICH-GCP guidelines
Intern at CROs like Covance, IQVIA
CRA → Clinical Project Manager
07
Healthcare & Medical Writing
Translate complex science into accessible content for doctors, patients and regulatory bodies.
Build strong scientific writing skills
Learn regulatory writing formats (CTD, SOP)
Freelance or join pharma comms teams
Medical Writer → Content Lead
08
Environmental Science & Policy
Work on biodiversity, conservation, pollution control and climate change with NGOs or government bodies.
M.Sc. Environmental Science / Ecology
Clear UPSC / State PSC (Environment track)
Work with MOEF, WWF, WRI India
Environment Officer → Policy Advisor
09
Biotech Entrepreneurship
Build startups solving healthcare, agriculture and diagnostics problems using biology and AI. The bold path.
Identify a real problem in Indian healthcare/agri
Learn basics of funding, MVP, validation
Apply to BIRAC, IIM incubators, IITD Foundation
Build → Pilot → Scale
10
Computational Biology / AI in Bio
The frontier of biology — use machine learning, deep learning and AI to solve protein folding, drug discovery and genomics.
Learn Python, ML basics, PyTorch/TensorFlow
Study AlphaFold, deep genomics papers
M.Tech Bioinformatics / CS + Bio double track
Research roles at Google DeepMind, EMBL, IISc
